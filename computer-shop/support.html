Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

What is your warranty coverage?

All NEXUS-built systems come with a comprehensive 3-year warranty covering all components and labor. We also offer optional extended warranties up to 5 years. The warranty covers hardware defects, manufacturing issues, and component failures. Custom builds using your own components may have different coverage terms.

How long does a custom build take?

Standard custom builds typically take 5-7 business days from order placement to shipping. This includes component sourcing, assembly, testing, and quality assurance. Rush builds (3-day turnaround) are available for an additional fee. Express shipping options are also available.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes! We offer flexible financing through multiple partners including 0% APR for 12 months on purchases over $1,500. We also accept major credit cards and offer monthly payment plans. Some restrictions may apply based on creditworthiness.

What operating system comes with my build?

All custom builds come pre-installed with Windows 11 Pro or your choice of Linux. The OS is pre-activated and ready to use right out of the box. You can request a different OS during configuration, and we'll install your preferred system at no additional cost.

Can I upgrade my system after purchase?

Absolutely! We recommend upgrades based on your usage. Common upgrades include adding more RAM, upgrading storage, or swapping GPU/CPU. For warranty purposes, upgrades performed by our team are recommended, though you can upgrade yourself. Warranty coverage applies to components we install.

What's your return/refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all custom builds if you're not completely satisfied. If there are any defects or issues, we'll either repair or replace your system at no cost. Return shipping is free within the continental US. Custom components may have different return terms based on manufacturer policies.

Do you offer setup and installation services?

Yes! We offer both in-home and remote setup services. Remote setup is complimentary with every purchase and includes driver installation, Windows configuration, and optimization. In-home setup services are available for an additional fee and include full installation, network setup, and personalized training.
